# Parent Dashboard Design Guidelines

The Parent Dashboard is a dedicated, data-rich hub that empowers parents to follow their child's developmental journey and language acquisition milestones.

## 1. Today's Overview
At the top of the dashboard, parents get an instant snapshot of today's learning outcomes:
- **Activity State**: Confirms if the child has engaged in any learning activity today (based on streak data).
- **Daily Mission Completion**: Tracks how many daily missions have been completed (e.g. `2/4 Completed`).
- **Streak Counter**: Shows the current streak size (flame count) to encourage consistency.

---

## 2. Learning Progress Metrics
The dashboard aggregates overall progress statistics dynamically to avoid stale data:
- **Completed Lessons**: Total unique lessons cleared.
- **Math Lessons**: Unique Math lessons completed.
- **Dialogue Lessons**: Unique Dialogue lessons completed.
- **Flashcards Studied**: Unique Flashcard decks marked learned.
- **Mascots Unlocked**: Total NPCs unlocked from scanning toy QR codes.

---

## 3. Recent Activity Timeline
Exposes a scrollable list of the **last 5 actions** performed by the child:
- Completing dialogue lessons (`📚`)
- Reviewing flashcard decks (`🎓`)
- Unlocking new Mascot friends (`👾`)
- Earning badges (`🏅`) or claiming mission rewards (`✨`)
Includes exact timestamps (hour:minute date/month) formatted locally.

---

## 4. Parenting Advice & Encouragement
Displays curated tips to guide parents on supportive learning practices at home:
1. **Short Sessions**: Keep learning sessions short and playful (5-10 minutes).
2. **No Pressure**: Do not force the child if they are not in the mood; try again when they are happy.
3. **Praise Effort**: Celebrate small steps like studying a single card to build self-esteem.
4. **Professional Consultation**: Recommend seeking advice from developmental specialists if parents notice any developmental concerns.

---

## 5. Safety & Medical Disclaimer
To maintain professional boundaries, the dashboard prominently features a disclaimer:
> [!CAUTION]
> This application is designed solely for educational companionship at home. The activities and progress metrics do not diagnose or treat any developmental disorders and are not a substitute for clinical evaluations, pediatric advice, or specialized clinical therapy.
